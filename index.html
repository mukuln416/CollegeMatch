About Our Application


The College Apps season of 12th grade is by far the most stressful part of high school. The Seniors need to decide what colleges they want to apply for and after that they will have to write an essay and pay an application fee for each university applied to. If the application gets rejected that is a complete waste of time writting the essay and a waste of money just to apply. Our app will help you save your money and time for writting essays. This app will help you decide what colleges you should apply to based on an algorithm that includes your acadamic stats, extracurriculars, and more.
